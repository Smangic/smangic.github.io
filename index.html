About Me

Haiyu is currently a master student of Electrical Engineering and Information Systems at The University of Tokyo. He received his B.Eng. degree in Communication Engineering from Southern University of Science and Technology in 2024. His research interests include flexible electronics, radio-frequency identification, and integrated sensing and communication.
